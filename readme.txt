Display customer ratings on your webpage and in the google search results. Meinungsmeister is the one click solution for your online reputation.

== Description ==

The Wordpress-Plugin from Meinungsmeister enables you to display your customer ratings on your webpage.
Google then recognizes your ratings and displays them in the google search results next to your webpage.

= This is Meinungsmeister =
Meinungsmeister is your partner for collecting, managing and displaying customer ratings.
Meinungsmeister shows your ratings wherever potential customers are searching:
* on your webpage
* in the Google search results
* in social networks
* in relevant german yellow pages (Das Örtliche, Gelbe Seiten, Das Telefonbuch, golocal, GoYellow)


= Why customer ratings are important for your business =
70% percent of all consumer-related decisions are based on online ratings. Online ratings are a reliable and trustworthy form of advertising for every small and medium sized enterprise (SME).

== Installation ==

You need the GoLocal Id of your Business to activate the plugin.
Search at https://www.golocal.de and check the url for the Id.
E.g. https://www.golocal.de/muenchen/internet/golocal-gmbh-co-kg-YUj9U/ has the Golocal Id YUj9U.

1. Upload the plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins/plugin-name` directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly.
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress
1. Use the Settings->Plugin Name screen to configure the plugin
